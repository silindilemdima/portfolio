About Me

I’m Silindile Kunene — a multidisciplinary designer and front-end developer who believes in the power of storytelling, culture, and purposeful creativity. With a Diploma in Art & Design from Central Johannesburg TVET College and a Certificate in Web Design from the University of Cape Town, I blend design thinking with development to create digital experiences that are both visually striking and user-focused.

Over the past decade, I’ve built a diverse body of work through professional and freelance collaborations with brands across South Africa and abroad. My expertise spans branding, visual identity, web design & development, UI/UX, and digital marketing — helping businesses and lifestyle brands turn ideas into design that feels intentional, human, and results-driven.

I’m deeply passionate about creating work that bridges creativity and functionality — where design meets code to craft meaningful connections between people and brands. Whether it’s building responsive websites, designing bold identities, or creating cohesive digital campaigns, I approach every project with curiosity, precision, and a commitment to excellence.
