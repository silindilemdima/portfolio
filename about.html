About Me

I’m Silindile Kunene, a multidisciplinary designer, front-end developer, and graphic designer driven by the power of storytelling, culture, and purposeful creativity. With a Diploma in Art & Design from Central Johannesburg TVET College and a Certificate in Web Design from the University of Cape Town, I combine visual design and web development to create seamless, impactful digital experiences.

Over the past decade, I’ve honed my craft through both professional roles and freelance projects, collaborating with a diverse range of clients locally and internationally. My work spans branding, visual identity, web development, UI/UX design, and digital marketing, helping businesses and lifestyle brands transform ideas into design that feels intentional and human.

I’m passionate about creating work that balances creativity and functionality, where design and code intersect to craft meaningful connections between people and brands. Whether it’s building engaging digital platforms, designing striking visual identities, or delivering cohesive marketing visuals, I approach every project with curiosity, precision, and a commitment to excellence.
