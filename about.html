About Me

I’m Silindile Kunene, a multidisciplinary designer, front end developer, and graphic designer driven by the power of storytelling, culture, and purposeful creativity. With a Diploma in Art & Design from Central Johannesburg TVET College and a Certificate in Web Design from the University of Cape Town, I combine visual design and web development to create seamless, impactful digital experiences.

My experience spans across branding, visual identity, and digital marketing, helping businesses and lifestyle brands bring their ideas to life through design that feels intentional and human. I’m passionate about work that balances creativity and functionality, design and code coming together to build meaningful connections between people and brands.
